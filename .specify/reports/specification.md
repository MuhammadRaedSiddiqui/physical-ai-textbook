# Functional Specification: Physical AI Textbook

**Project Overview:**
A comprehensive digital textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, hosted as a Docusaurus site.

**Curriculum Structure (13 Weeks):**
The book is divided into 4 core modules + a Capstone.

* **Module 1: ROS 2 Foundations (Weeks 1-4)**
    * Week 1: Introduction to Physical AI & Setup
    * Week 2: ROS 2 Architecture (Nodes, Topics, Services)
    * Week 3: Advanced Communication (Actions, Parameters)
    * Week 4: Navigation 2 (Nav2) & SLAM

* **Module 2: Simulation Mastery (Weeks 5-7)**
    * Week 5: Gazebo Classic vs. Ignition
    * Week 6: Unity for Robotics (URDF Import)
    * Week 7: Sim-to-Real Transfer Basics

* **Module 3: NVIDIA Ecosystem (Weeks 8-10)**
    * Week 8: Introduction to NVIDIA Isaac Sim
    * Week 9: Isaac Gym & Reinforcement Learning
    * Week 10: Jetson Hardware Optimization

* **Module 4: The Brain - VLA Models (Weeks 11-12)**
    * Week 11: Vision-Language-Action Models (Overview)
    * Week 12: Training a VLA for Robotic Manipulation

* **Week 13: Capstone Project**
    * Building a "Tidy-Up" Robot in Simulator.

**Technical Requirements:**
* Each week must be a separate Markdown file in `docs/`.
* Code snippets must be provided for Python and C++.
